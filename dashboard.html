About MoEngage

MoEngage is an intelligent customer engagement platform that allows businesses to deliver personalized experiences to their customers across multiple channels.

Built for the customer-obsessed marketer, MoEngage creates a unified view of your customer across touchpoints, and optimizes the customer journey across the web, mobile, and email.

Customer Analytics

Get deep insights into customer behavior with advanced analytics and reporting tools.

Omnichannel Engagement

Reach customers across email, SMS, push notifications, in-app messages, and web push.

Personalization

Deliver personalized experiences using AI-powered recommendations and dynamic content.

Marketing Automation

Create sophisticated customer journeys with drag-and-drop workflow builder.

A/B Testing

Optimize campaigns with comprehensive A/B testing capabilities.

Real-time Segmentation

Create dynamic customer segments based on real-time behavior and attributes.
